Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: January 2025

If you wish to request the deletion of your data associated with any OverPowered application, including Receipts Wasabi n8n, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Send an email to:

overpowered.compliance@gmail.com

Include the following:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address associated with the app
  • A brief note requesting deletion of all stored data

Data will be deleted within 7 business days.
